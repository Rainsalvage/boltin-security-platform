🔒 Stolen Gadget Recovery Process

Complete Step-by-Step Guide for Device Recovery

Recovery Process Overview

The BOLTIN Security Platform follows a systematic approach to maximize the chances of recovering stolen devices. This process involves immediate action, investigation, community assistance, and legal procedures working together to return your device safely.

Time is Critical

The first 24-48 hours after theft are crucial. Quick action significantly increases recovery chances from 15% to 75%.

1

🚨 Immediate Emergency Response

Within 0-2 Hours

Critical first actions when your device is stolen:

  • Report to BOLTIN Platform: Log into your account and mark device as stolen
  • Contact Police: File official police report immediately (get case number)
  • Remote Actions: Enable remote lock, wipe, and tracking if available
  • Network Notifications: Alert contacts about theft to prevent social engineering
  • Insurance Claims: Contact insurance provider to start claim process
  • Account Security: Change passwords for all accounts accessed on the device

2

🔍 Investigation & Tracking

2-24 Hours

Systematic investigation and tracking phase:

  • CCTV Analysis: Police review security footage from incident location
  • Digital Forensics: Track last known location and network activity
  • Witness Interviews: Collect statements from potential witnesses
  • Pawn Shop Alerts: Notify local pawn shops and resellers
  • Online Monitoring: Monitor online marketplaces for device listings
  • IMEI Blocking: Carrier blocks device from network access

Investigation Timeline:

2-4 Hours: CCTV Review

Security footage analysis from theft location and surrounding areas

4-8 Hours: Digital Tracking

GPS, WiFi, and cellular data analysis for device location

8-24 Hours: Market Monitoring

Active scanning of online and physical resale channels

3

🎯 Active Recovery Operations

1-7 Days

Multi-channel recovery efforts:

  • Sting Operations: Police conduct controlled buys from suspected sellers
  • Location Raids: Search warrants executed at identified locations
  • Public Appeals: Media campaigns for public assistance
  • Reward Programs: Financial incentives for information leading to recovery
  • Undercover Operations: Plain-clothes officers monitor known theft hotspots
  • Community Patrols: Local security teams increased presence

📱 Device Discovery Scenarios

Devices are typically recovered through: Police raids (45%), Public tips (25%), Pawn shop finds (20%), or Suspect arrests (10%).

4

⚖️ Legal Process & Recovery

1-30 Days

Legal procedures and device return:

  • Evidence Processing: Device held as evidence during investigation
  • Ownership Verification: Proof of ownership using BOLTIN registration
  • Suspect Prosecution: Legal proceedings against perpetrators
  • Device Release: Formal return process once legal requirements met
  • Condition Assessment: Technical evaluation and data recovery if needed
  • Case Closure: Final report and case documentation

🔐 Ownership Verification Process

BOLTIN registration serves as official proof of ownership, streamlining the legal return process and reducing recovery time by up to 80%.

5

🎉 Device Return & Restoration

Final Step

Safe return and restoration process:

  • Secure Handover: Device returned at police station with proper documentation
  • Condition Verification: Technical inspection for damage or tampering
  • Data Recovery: Professional data restoration if device was damaged
  • Security Reset: Complete device wipe and security reconfiguration
  • Status Update: BOLTIN platform updated to show device as recovered
  • Case Documentation: Complete recovery report for insurance/legal purposes
